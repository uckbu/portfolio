About Me

Hello! I'm John Balci, a high school student at TJHSST with a passion for web development and art. I have a strong foundation in Java, Python, HTML, and JavaScript, which I leverage to create engaging and interactive websites.

As an artist, I find joy in expressing my creativity through various mediums. I believe that the combination of technology and art can lead to immersive interactions between advancements and art.

When I'm not coding or creating art, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading about the latest trends in web development, or collaborating with fellow enthusiasts on exciting projects.

I'm constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow, both as a developer and as an individual. I'm excited about the endless possibilities that the world of technology and art has to offer.
